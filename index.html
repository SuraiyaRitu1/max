Our personalised iron-on clothing labels are a quick and easy way to identify clothing and school uniforms. No more hours spent sewing labels - our nametags apply in seconds directly to fabric and are designed to be comfortable with no irritation to your child’s skin. Waterproof and safe for both washing machines and tumble dryers, our iron-on labels are built to last. In fact, we’re so confident in their quality that we offer a 10-year washing guarantee.

1. Peel the label from the backing sheet, place on the garment and cover with the silicone sheet provided.
2. Set your iron to the highest temperature suitable for the fabric (no steam).
3. Press firmly over the label for 10–20 seconds.
4. Remove the silicone sheet, allow the label to cool, and check that all edges are fully bonded. If needed, iron for a few more seconds.

Important information
- Always follow the garment’s care instructions.
- Do not apply labels to fabrics that are not suitable for ironing.
- Always use the silicone sheet when applying labels.
- Avoid direct ironing over the label after application.
- When ironing garments, use a protective layer over the label to prevent damage. If you do not have one, avoid direct contact between the iron and the label.
